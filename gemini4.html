Frequently Asked Questions

What's your approach to accessibility?

We believe the web should be for everyone. We build to WCAG 2.1 AA standards, ensuring our products are usable by people with disabilities through semantic HTML, ARIA attributes, and keyboard navigability.

How do you handle project management?

We use a dedicated project manager for every project. Communication is key, so we provide a shared Slack channel, regular check-in meetings, and a project dashboard (usually in Notion or Jira) for full transparency.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing support, maintenance, and feature development to ensure your digital product continues to evolve and perform optimally after launch.
