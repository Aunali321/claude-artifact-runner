Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while more complex applications can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you work with clients internationally?
Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration and we have clients across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. We use various tools to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.
What technologies do you specialize in?
We work with a wide range of modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue, Angular), PHP, Python, Node.js, and various CMS platforms like WordPress. We select the best technology stack based on your specific project requirements.
Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital assets continue to perform optimally after launch. We can also provide training to help your team manage the solutions we create.
