Common Questions.

What types of clients do you work with?

We partner with a diverse range of clients, from ambitious startups to established enterprise companies. Our ideal partner is anyone who values high-quality design and robust engineering, and is looking for a collaborative, long-term creative partnership.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on the scope. A branding project might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex web application could take 4-6 months or more. After our initial 'Immerse' phase, we provide a detailed project roadmap with clear timelines.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and retainer models for ongoing work. Every project is unique, so we provide a custom proposal after our initial discovery call. We focus on delivering value and a clear ROI, not just hours.

How do we start a project with Infinitix?

The best way to start is by filling out the contact form below. Tell us a bit about your project and your goals. We'll get back to you within one business day to schedule a discovery call and see if we're a good fit.
